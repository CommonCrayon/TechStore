TechStore Description:

TechStore is a retail application that specialises in offering
a wide range of consumer electronics, computer hardware, and
related products. With a focus on technology and gadgets,
TechStore provides customers with access to a diverse selection
of items such as computers, laptops, gaming gear, peripherals,
and accessories. The application serves as a one-stop
destination for tech enthusiasts and professionals seeking the
latest and most innovative products in the world of electronics
and computing. TechStore also provides various shopping options
to cater to the needs of its user base.


TechStore Roles:

TechStore consists of the following roles:
1. Admin: This role has full control over users, orders, and items. Admins can create, modify, and delete user accounts, orders, and items in the store.

2. Employee: Employees have the privilege to view all users, orders, and items in the store. They do not have the authority to make changes to the data but can access and analyze it.

3. Customer: Customers are allowed to purchase items from the store. They can browse the available products, add items to their cart, and complete the purchase process.

4. Packager: Packagers are responsible for packaging orders and marking them as complete. They ensure that orders are correctly prepared for shipping and update their status as ready for delivery.

Please note that these roles are defined to help manage TechStore effectively, with each role having distinct responsibilities and permissions.
